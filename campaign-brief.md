## Brand DNA Summary
Alias Shortcut Bible speaks in a direct, bold, no-fluff voice (bold 8/10, expert-focused, efficiency-obsessed) built for the professional Autodesk Alias user who values precision and zero wasted time. The visual identity is unmistakably technical and premium: a near-black #080808 background, glowing #FFD700 gold accents, flat product-centered design with no people and no stock photography. The target audience is automotive surface and industrial designers (22-40) at OEM studios or freelancing, whose core pain is hunting through menus for commands and whose aspiration is to model as fast as senior OEM designers and find any command in 3 seconds.

## Audit Context
No audit data (`ADS-AUDIT-REPORT.md` or `*-audit-results.md`) was found in the working directory. These concepts are built from the brand profile and supplied campaign parameters alone, addressing the audience's stated pain points directly rather than measured performance gaps. For more targeted, fatigue-aware concepts in future cycles, run `/ads audit` first. Because this is a Product Launch playbook on a pain-aware professional audience, all five concepts use the **PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)** copy framework, sequencing the menu-hunting pain into the $12 instant-download solution.

## Campaign Concepts

### Concept 1: The Menu Hunt
**Hypothesis:** Pain-aware Alias users instantly recognize the frustration of digging through nested menus, so leading with that exact moment will stop the scroll and earn the click.
**Primary Message:** You are still hunting through menus while senior designers fire commands from muscle memory.
**Tone:** Bold 8/10, expert-focused, efficiency-obsessed, no-fluff (per brand voice).
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)
**Visual Direction A (Product):** Dark editorial shot of an abstract nested-menu cascade dissolving into a clean gold-edged cheat-sheet card. Product mode, dark stack with a single glowing gold accent trail.
**Visual Direction B (Abstract):** Flat illustration of a tangled gold pathway looping endlessly through menu-shaped blocks against #080808, conveying wasted clicks. Abstract mode.
**Target Platforms:** Meta Feed (1080x1080) — square stops the scroll and gives room for a problem-first hook overlay.
**CTA:** Get Instant Access
**Addresses:** General brand awareness (no audit) — targets stated pain "hunting through menus for commands."

### Concept 2: 3 Seconds Flat
**Hypothesis:** The aspiration "find any command in 3 seconds" is a concrete, measurable promise that productivity-driven designers will respond to far better than vague speed claims.
**Primary Message:** Every command, found in 3 seconds — not 30 menu clicks.
**Tone:** Bold 8/10, efficiency-obsessed, precise, technical.
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)
**Visual Direction A (Product):** Product hero of the cheat sheet mid-flip, a glowing gold stopwatch-arc sweeping across the page edge implying instant lookup. Product mode, dark with gold motion glow.
**Visual Direction B (Abstract):** Abstract flat composition of a gold arc snapping from a slow scattered cluster of dots to a single tight point of light. Abstract mode, motion metaphor.
**Target Platforms:** Meta Feed (1080x1080) — clean center frame holds the speed metaphor while top/bottom carry the time-saving hook.
**CTA:** Get Instant Access
**Addresses:** General brand awareness (no audit) — targets aspiration "find any command in 3 seconds."

### Concept 3: The OEM Standard
**Hypothesis:** Authority and peer-credibility ("what senior OEM designers use") taps the audience aspiration to model like the best, lending the product instant professional legitimacy.
**Primary Message:** The shortcut system senior OEM surface designers rely on, now in one sheet.
**Tone:** Bold 8/10, premium, expert-focused, traditional-innovative 6/10.
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)
**Visual Direction A (Product):** Premium editorial product shot of the cheat sheet presented like a credential on a dark technical surface, gold seal-like accent glow. Product mode, authoritative dark premium.
**Visual Direction B (Abstract):** Abstract minimal composition of stacked gold tiers ascending to a single capstone line, evoking mastery and rank. Abstract mode.
**Target Platforms:** Meta Feed (1080x1080) — square center reads as a premium credential card with margin for authority copy.
**CTA:** Get Instant Access
**Addresses:** General brand awareness (no audit) — targets aspiration "model like senior OEM designers / look professional at work."

### Concept 4: Inside the Bible
**Hypothesis:** Showing the actual workflow-grouped, color-coded content reduces purchase risk for a low-ticket impulse buy by proving the product is real, organized, and immediately usable.
**Primary Message:** 200+ shortcuts, 14 illustrated pages, grouped by workflow and color-coded.
**Tone:** Bold 8/10, rational-leaning 4/10, technical, no-fluff.
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)
**Visual Direction A (Product):** Flat-lay product mockup of the multi-page cheat sheet fanned out with the A3 poster behind, color-coded category bands glowing in gold and green. Product mode, content-forward.
**Visual Direction B (UI/Web):** Abstract dark UI silhouette of grouped shortcut tiles in a clean grid, color-coded blocks without legible labels. UI/Web mode.
**Target Platforms:** Meta Feed (1080x1080) — square showcases the full content spread; lower band reserved for the offer line.
**CTA:** Get Instant Access
**Addresses:** General brand awareness (no audit) — targets pain "not knowing advanced shortcuts," de-risks the launch with proof of substance.

### Concept 5: $12 vs Your Week
**Hypothesis:** Framing the trivial $12 against the recurring hours lost to slow lookups makes the value math undeniable for a price-conscious direct-response audience, especially at 73% off.
**Primary Message:** $12 once, versus the hours you lose to slow modeling every single week.
**Tone:** Bold 8/10, direct, efficiency-obsessed, rational 4/10.
**Copy Framework:** PAS (Problem > Agitate > Solution)
**Visual Direction A (Product):** Product shot of the cheat sheet beside a glowing gold price-tag accent, dark premium contrast emphasizing the slashed value. Product mode, value-forward dark.
**Visual Direction B (Abstract):** Abstract split composition — a heavy draining gold sand pile (lost hours) on one side, a single small bright coin-point on the other. Abstract mode, value metaphor.
**Target Platforms:** Meta Feed (1080x1080) — square split layout contrasts cost vs. loss with copy zone for the 73%-off line.
**CTA:** Claim 73% Off
**Addresses:** General brand awareness (no audit) — targets the price/value decision and the limited launch discount.
